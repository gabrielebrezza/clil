Methods of Hydrogen Production

Hydrogen can be produced through various methods:

  • Electrolysis: Using electricity to split water into hydrogen and oxygen. When powered by renewable energy, this method produces green hydrogen with minimal environmental impact.
  • Steam Methane Reforming (SMR): Extracting hydrogen from natural gas. This process is more common but produces carbon emissions unless combined with carbon capture technologies.

Advancements in technology are making hydrogen production more efficient and environmentally friendly, ensuring its role in a sustainable energy future.
